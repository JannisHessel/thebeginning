A HTML and CSS Tour through the Bavarian Forest

The Reservoir Bavarian Forest is the oldest nature reservoir in Germany. In its heart,at the czech border, there is a special mountain raising his bald head above the surrounding. On the path to the Top of this unique mountain we will encounter a lot of the recent Nature history of the Area and some older phenomena.

Hint:in the bottom left corner you can hide all elements to look at the background!

The about 10 km long tour will take us along a river that was used for wood drafting to the reservoir at the top of it, which is now a mountain lake biotope. Past that we will go along the steep hillside towards the Teufelsloch, a rocky canyon in the side of the mountain. Shortly before the raise to the summit there is an elevated plane which did undergo quite a big vegetation change recently. The raise to the summit is called Himmelsleiter- a staircase consisting of the nature rocks. The summit is a Giant sea of rocks that gives the Mountain its unique look and makes for a great unblocked view of everything around.
